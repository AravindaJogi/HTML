Alva’s College which has a well-established Centre for Post Graduate Studies and Research along with Graduate Programs. Alva’s is a unique educational institution that embodies all academic and cultural characters. Alva’s facilitates each student in their overall growth to link them closely with the corporate sector, higher education and research, administrative services (public sector), professional courses and entrepreneurship. Application forms can be obtained from the Admission officers on payment of Rs. 150/- in cash or by DD drawn in favour of “The Chairman, Alva’s Education Foundation ® , Moodbidri” . Application forms filled in, complete with all the information asked for, in the candidate’s own hand writing, should be registered in Admission Office by the due date with registration fee Rs. 150/-. Applications received by post will be registered only if a DD of Rs. 150/- payable to the “The Chairman, Alva’s Education Foundation®, Moodbidri” is enclosed. An acknowledgement will be sent only if a self addressed post card is enclosed. Students who do not appear for the interview on the specified day are liable to lose the seat provisionally allotted to them. Students who are finally selected after the interview should pay the fees immediately. The fees will not be refunded under any circumstances. Selected students who do not pay the fees or after paying the fees do not attend the class for five consecutive days, will forfeit their seats. Original documents should be submitted to the college office while paying the fees.
